Our Story

Design is
not art.
Design is
intention.

FORMA was founded in 2016 by designers who believed great design should be indistinguishable from magic — effortless for the user, invisible in its complexity, and impossible to ignore.

We've grown from a 3-person studio in San Francisco's Dogpatch neighborhood into a 28-person creative collective with offices in New York and Amsterdam. Our work spans brand identity, digital products, motion design, and environmental graphics.

We don't do templates. We don't do "good enough." We do the kind of work that makes clients call us a year later to tell us how much it changed things.

Our Principles

What We Believe

01 / Craft
Obsessive Precision
We sweat every kern, every spacing unit, every pixel. Not because clients always notice — but because they feel it. The difference between good and great is invisible until it isn't.
02 / Strategy
Design With Purpose
Beautiful design that doesn't work is decoration. We start every project by asking what success looks like, then build toward it. Form follows function, always.
03 / Courage
Bold Creative Choices
The safest choice is the most dangerous one. We challenge briefs, push back on convention, and advocate for the unexpected solution — because that's what becomes iconic.
